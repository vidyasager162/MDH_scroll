ABOUT

This is our DBMS project

We (Vidyasager GR & Sairaj Patro) the students of SSSIHL have chosen our DBMS Project to compile the database of all the faculty (teaching and non-teaching) and students of our MDH Campus.

Apart from being a Database of Faculties and Students, it will also lead to making a software that generates timetable and exam schedules based on the details in the Database, and to allocate rooms in campus and hostel. Additionally these details could be used to analyse the students joining the institute and make data analysis based on them.

The Base Idea: A Repository of all the Faculty.

Final Result: A web-interface (and / or) python-based software that allows users to access details of faculty and students and also the administration to generate timetables and test schedules.



PROBLEM STATEMENT

When a staff(Teaching/Non-teaching) joins the institute, he is provided with his department details. Once he is in campus, he is requested to submit certain details and documents. These details are entered into the system to keep a record of all the teaching and non-teaching faculties of the campus.
When a student joins the institute, he is provided with his accomodation(hostel) details, and roll number and other details based on his course details. Once he is in campus, he is requested to submit certain documents. These details are entered into the system to keep a record of all the students of the campus.

(This rendition of the Database will encompass data of faculty and students of the MDH campus specifically) But in essence, this system can be implemented across all the campuses. Eg: We can also create a BRN Campus Database.

Details of the faculty that will be provided by them are as follows:
  1. Teacher Name
  2. DOB (Date of Birth)
  3. Address
  4. Qualifications
  5. Experience
  6. Teaching Areas
  7. Phone No.
  8. Email ID
  9. Blood Group

Additional Details will be added to the Database at a later stage along with the given details by the faculty/staff.
They are Namely:
  1. Department
  2. Teacher ID (Unique)
  3. Courses or Subjects to be taken (Could be multiple) (Subject code will be given)
  4. Room Number (If he is a residing faculty)
  5. Post (Position) Eg: Associate Professor
  6. Desk No. (Unique)
  7. DOJ (Date of Joining)

Details of the students that will be provided by them are as follows:
  1. Student Name
  2. DOB (Date of Birth)
  3. Address
  4. Parent Details (Name, Occupation, Qualification, Annual Income)
  5. Phone No.
  6. Email ID
  7. Blood Group
  8. Course Details
  9. Religion
  10. Caste (If applicable)
  11. Vaccination Details (Boolean)

Additional Details will be added to the Database at a later stage along with the given details by the students.
They are namely:
  1. Reg No.
  2. Room No.
  3. Cupboard No.
  4. Room Leader (Boolean)

The following data will be residing (a few more attributes could be added at a later stage) in the Database for MDH Campus and can be used to view about the details of each teacher and student.
